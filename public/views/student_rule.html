Instruction

➣ All the complaints relating to a perticular department shall first be addressed to head of that departments whose composition     shall be as follows:
  • HOD
  • Librarian
  • Controller of examination
  • placement officier
  • Accountant
  • Student section

➣     The above committee shall be approved by the statutory body of institution

➣     The complaints not related to departments and the grievances not resolved at the department level shall be reffered to the institutional level, whose composition shall be as follows:

  • Registrar
  • Pro-Vice Chancellor/Dean

➣     In case of grievances not resolved at the institute level shall be reffered to vice chancellor

➣     Any student aggrieved by the decision at the department level or institute level may within in a period of six days prefer an appeal to the vice chancellor

➣     In case of any false or frivolous complaint, the vice chancellor may recommend appropriate action against the complaint

➣    Only DBATU college students can lodge the grievance
